The East African Community consists of 6 countries:

Uganda

Independence: October 9, 1962

Capital City: Kampala

President: Yoweri Museveni

Famous for: Source of the Nile River, diverse wildlife, vibrant cultural heritage

Kenya

Independence: December 12, 1963

Capital City: Nairobi

President: Uhuru Kenyatta

Famous for: Maasai Mara, Mount Kenya, rich wildlife, long-distance runners

Tanzania

Independence: December 9, 1961 / December 10, 1963

Capital City: Dodoma / Dar es Salaam

President: Samia Suluhu Hassan

Famous for: Mount Kilimanjaro, Serengeti National Park, Zanzibar beaches, Great Rift Valley

Rwanda

Independence: July 1, 1962

Capital City: Kigali

President: Paul Kagame

Famous for: 1994 genocide, mountain gorillas, reconciliation efforts
